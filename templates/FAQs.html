2 For whom are seats reserved in PMPML buses?

There are seats reserved for women, the elderly, the visually impaired and the physically disabled.


3 Does PMPML provide any tour of the city?

Yes, we provide 'Pune Darshan' and 'Pimpri Chinchwad Darshan' city darshan services.
